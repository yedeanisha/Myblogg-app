Welcome to The Glutton Jurnol !This is created to celebrate how nourishing, fun, and delicious cooking with seasonal fruits and vegetables can be. I hope that the recipes here inspire you to try cooking a new vegetable, or try cooking a familiar vegetable in a new way. You’ll find a host of inventive vegetarian and Non-vegetarian recipes for breakfast, lunch, and dinner for you. We have guides to working with vegetables ranging from spaghetti squash to asparagus and tips for cooking plant-based basics like quinoa, rice, and lentils. The New York Times said of The Glotton Jurnol, “Happiness itself is tossed into every bowl,” while Bon Appétit hailed it as, “the most beautiful cookbook we’ve ever seen.” The Glutton Jurnol Every Day, was featured in The New York Times Book Review’s 2019 Summer Reading List. We have also developed recipes for KitchenAid, Whole Foods, Le Creuset, Anthropologie, and Crate & Barrel, among others.
